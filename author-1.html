Prof. Yuni Kusuma Arumsari, Ph.D.

Professor of Political Science

Political Science Governance Public Policy

Biography

Prof. Yuni Kusuma Arumsari is a distinguished scholar in the field of Political Science with over 20 years of research experience in governance and public policy. She obtained her Ph.D. from the University of Melbourne, Australia, with a dissertation on "Decentralization and Democratic Governance in Southeast Asia".

Currently serving as the Head of Political Science Department at Universitas Brawijaya, she has published extensively in international journals and served as a consultant for various government agencies on policy formulation and institutional reform.

Her research interests include:

  • State reconstruction and institutional development
  • Decentralization and local governance
  • Public policy analysis
  • Democratization processes in Southeast Asia
